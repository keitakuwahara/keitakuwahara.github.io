Keita Kuwahara

Profile

I am a graduate student at the Graduate School of Economics, The University of Tokyo, with an interest in microeconomic theory. I earned my BA in Economics from the University of Tokyo in 2025. Currently, I work as a research assistant at the Market Design Center, The University of Tokyo, and the ERATO Kojima Market Design Project.
